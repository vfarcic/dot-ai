Generating skills from the `prd-full` prompt now produces a complete `SKILL.md` with its full instruction body. Previously its `prdNumber` and `mode` arguments were marked `required: true`, so static skill generation (which calls `prompts/get` with no arguments) failed with `400 Missing required arguments` and wrote a metadata-only, body-less skill. Both arguments are now `required: false` — the prompt body still enforces them at invocation time — matching sibling prompts like `prd-start`.
